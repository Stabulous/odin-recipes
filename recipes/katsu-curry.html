Chicken Katsu Curry

Description

Traditional Japanese dish consisting katsu style chicken with rice and a Japanese curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Potato
  • Various ingredients to make the sauce
  • Chicken
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Cornflour

Steps

  1. Dice onion, garlic and ginger. Lightly fry in pot until fragrant.
  2. Roughly chop carrots and potatoes. Set aside for now.
  3. Add various ingredients for sauce and bring to boil.
  4. Add roughly chopped carrots and potatoes and bring back up to boil. Reduce to simmer for 20 minutes, stirring regularly.
  5. Once carrots and potatoes have softened, add cornflour slurry until sauce is desired thickness.
  6. Meanwhile, flour egg & breadcrumb the chicken.
  7. Fry in hot oil until golden brown on both sides.
  8. Serve on rice with pickled ginger as a side.

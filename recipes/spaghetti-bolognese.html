Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Italian favourite consisting pasta of your choice, spaghetti in this case, and tomato based pasta sauce.

Ingredients

  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Minced meat (beef or pork)
  • Brown onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Red wine
  • Full-cream milk
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. 3/4 fill pot of water to bring to boil whilst preparing sauce.
  2. Dice onion, carrot, and celery. Heat EVOO on medium-high heat and add diced vegetables. Fry until vegetables have softened and onion is translucent.
  3. Add minced meat to the pan and fry until brown.
  4. Add red wine and cook off.
  5. Add tinned tomatoes, tomato paste, and dash of milk. Stir to mix and bring to simmer. Reduce heat to medium and reduce sauce down to desired consistency. Stir every 5mins to prevent burning. Taste frequently and adjust with salt/pepper as necessary.
  6. Whilst the sauce is reducing, add salt to the pot of boiling water, then add spaghetti whilst stirring to prevent clumping.
  7. Cook spaghetti until aldente (slight crunch) - approximately 8-10mins. Follow packaging guidelines depending on brand of spaghetti.
  8. Drain spaghetti and serve into bowl. Add pasta sauce to the bowl and dress with basil.

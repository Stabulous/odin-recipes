Thyme and Ricotta Gnocchi

Description

Gnocchi infused with thyme and ricotta fried in butter and finished with garden peas and parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Thyme
  • Ricotta
  • Potatoes
  • Plain flour
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Peas

Steps

  1. 3/4 fill pot of water to bring to boil.
  2. Par-boil potatoes - slightly less cooked than mashed potatoes.
  3. Drain potatoes and allow to cool.
  4. Once cool, break up with a fork and mix with flour, thyme and ricotta.
  5. Fold into a dough taking care not to knead - only lightly mix otherwise too much gluten will form.
  6. Separate dough into 4 portions and roll into thin sausages - approx. 3cm diameter.
  7. Flour your knife and cut into small bite sized pieces.
  8. Flour your finger and lightly press on each piece to create a pillow like shape. The indent will hold more sauce also.
  9. Boil the gnocchi until floating on the surface. Transfer straight into hot pan and lightly fry on each side.
  10. Once golden brown, add butter, peas and parmesan and fry for a further 1-2 minutes until golden, melted and fragrant.
